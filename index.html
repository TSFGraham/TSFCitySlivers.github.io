Nevada City, City Slivers Program

This is a map depicting the City of Nevada City owned parcels and “Slivers”. The City Sliver Program is aimed to identify, characterize, promote stewardship, track status and complete management practices to reduce both fuel density and fire risk for each “Sliver”.
